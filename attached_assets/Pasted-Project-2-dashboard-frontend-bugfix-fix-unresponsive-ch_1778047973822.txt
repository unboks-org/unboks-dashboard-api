Bug:
Sidebar channel rows like WhatsApp and Email become unresponsive after switching between them.

Observed:
1. Click WhatsApp.
2. Click Email.
3. Click WhatsApp again.
4. It sometimes does not switch back / filter does not update.

Expected:
- Clicking WhatsApp must always show WhatsApp conversations.
- Clicking Email must always show Email conversations.
- Clicking Inbox must always clear the channel filter and show all conversations.
- Re-clicking any sidebar item must be reliable, even when the route is already the same page.

Likely cause:
The sidebar items navigate to the same route `/`, and because route does not change, the active channel state is not always updated.
A previous fix made Inbox call onNavSelect, but channel rows may still not force state update every time.

3. Email click sets active channel/filter to Email every time.
4. Instagram/Facebook/Messenger/X/TikTok clicks do the same.
5. Inbox click clears active channel/filter every time.
6. Active visual state must update correctly.
7. Filtered conversation list must update immediately.
8. Counts remain correct.
9. No full page reload.
10. Do not change backend/API.
