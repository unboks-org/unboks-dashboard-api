   Use subtle off-white or grouped surfaces if appropriate.
2. Logo area:

   - Keep logo visible and premium.
   - Avoid tiny tagline being clipped or awkward.
3. Status area:

   - Make it look like a compact status pill/card, not random text.
4. Primary navigation:
   - Inbox and Escalations should be grouped clearly.
   - Active state should be elegant: soft blue background + blue text/icon.

5. Channels:
   - Section label should be clean.

6. Bookings/Orders:

7. Sign out:

8. Scrollbar:

   - Sidebar content should scroll cleanly without visual ugliness.
9. Mobile:
   - Drawer width should feel intentional.
   - Tap targets should be comfortable.
   - No horizontal overflow.
   - Footer/sign out should be reachable.
10. Desktop:
   - Width should remain practical.
   - No wasted huge spaces.
   - Sidebar should not feel cramped.
