Issue:
Settings currently has a “Feature Visibility” section with:
- AI Suggest Reply toggle
- Email Notifications toggle

This is not right for the customer dashboard:
1. We do not need customers to hide sidebar sections here.
2. AI Suggest Reply toggle is unnecessary. The AI icon/action will exist in the UI; if the user wants to use it they can, if not they ignore it.
3. Email escalation notifications should be mandatory/default, not an optional toggle.
4. Customers should be able to choose optional direct messenger channels where escalation alerts are sent.

Product requirement:
Escalation notifications:
- Email is default/mandatory.
- Optional direct messenger notification channels:
  - WhatsApp
  - Messenger
  - Telegram
- For each optional channel, customer can toggle it on and enter where notifications should be sent.
- Destination can be:
  - phone number
  - username
  - email
  depending on the channel.
- Example: The connected WhatsApp Business account may be used for customer messages, but the business owner may want escalation alerts sent to a private WhatsApp number or the boss’s number.
- This setting is about where escalation alerts go, not which customer channel is connected.
