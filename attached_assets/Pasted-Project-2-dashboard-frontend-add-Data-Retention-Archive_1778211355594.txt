Feature:
Add a Data Retention & Archive settings area.

Product reason:
Clients may stay with Unboks for years and receive thousands or millions of messages.
We cannot keep every old message in the active inbox forever.

Best practice:
- Active inbox stays clean.
- Old inactive conversations are archived.
- Very old archived messages can be deleted or anonymized based on a retention policy.
- Approved AI learnings can remain even if the original raw messages are archived/deleted.

Use Refero/Repro:
Use compact premium SaaS settings references before designing.
References should include:
- Google Workspace retention settings
- Slack retention settings
- Intercom data retention/admin settings
- Front shared inbox archive settings
- Zendesk data retention settings
- Linear compact settings UI

If Refero/Repro is not connected, stop and report clearly before editing.

Required controls:

1. Active inbox retention
Label:
Archive inactive conversations after

Dropdown options:
- 30 days
- 60 days
- 90 days
- 180 days
- Never automatically archive

Default:
90 days

Explanation, small muted text:
Inactive conversations leave the daily inbox but remain searchable in the archive.

2. Archive retention
Label:
Keep archived conversations for

Dropdown options:
- 12 months
- 24 months
- 36 months
- 5 years
- Never delete automatically

Default:
24 months

Explanation:
Archived conversations stay available for history, disputes, and customer context.

3. End-of-retention action
Label:
After the archive period

Dropdown options:
- Anonymize customer data
- Delete messages permanently
- Keep forever

Default:
Anonymize customer data

Explanation:
Anonymizing keeps useful statistics while removing personal customer details.

4. AI learning rule
Checkbox:
Keep approved AI learnings after original messages are archived or deleted

Default:
On

Explanation:
Approved answers and business knowledge can remain available to the AI without keeping the full private conversation forever.

- 36 months

24 months
